Our Story

Founded in 2024, Ai-oko Fabrics has been at the forefront of bringing premium Nigerian textiles, bags, and footwear to fashion enthusiasts and designers worldwide.

We source our products directly from skilled artisans and reputable manufacturers across Nigeria, ensuring authenticity and supporting local craftsmanship. Each item in our collection tells a story of cultural heritage and artistic expression.

At Ai-oko, we believe that fashion is more than just clothing – it's a statement of identity, culture, and personal style. That's why we're dedicated to providing exceptional quality products that empower our customers to express themselves authentically.

Fabric Care Instructions

Proper care ensures your fabrics maintain their beauty and longevity.

Chingavy Care

Hand wash cold, dry flat, iron low.

Lace Care

Dry clean recommended. Lay flat to dry.

Ankara Care

Machine wash cold, tumble dry low.

Aso Oke Care

Dry clean only. Store away from sunlight.
